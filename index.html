About

Dr. Amit Yadav is Chief Pediatric Ophthalmologist in Prakash Netra Kendra, Gomti Nagar, Lucknow.

He also served two years in EyeQ Hospital as Senior Consultant in Department of Pediatric Ophthalmology and Occuloplasty at Gomti Nagar, Aliganj, Rajaji Puram, Saraswati Puram, Vijay Nagar branches in Lucknow.

He has experience of more than 26,000 surgeries of various types including but not limited to Microphaco (Cataract) Surgery, Pediatric Eye Surgery, Squint Surgery, Nystagmus Surgery, Lid Surgery, DCR, Ptosis and other Oculoplasty Surgeries, Glucoma Surgery, Amblyopia Management in Children.

Dr. Amit Yadav performed Live surgery in front of thousand as audience in Chitrakoot in 2014 (under NCB program).

Dr. Amit Yadav received prestigious Dr. Ramesh Krishnan Award during his fellowship in 2014.
